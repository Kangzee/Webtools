Why use our Free Password Generator?

Cybersecurity experts recommend using long, randomly generated passwords containing a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. Our tool uses your browser's local cryptography to instantly generate strong passwords. We do not store or track any passwords generated on this site.

What makes a password strong?

  • Length: Minimum of 12-16 characters.
  • Complexity: Uses numbers, symbols, and mixed casing.
  • Uniqueness: Never reuse the same password across multiple websites.

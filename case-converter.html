About the Case Converter Tool

Have you ever typed a whole document only to realize you left the Caps Lock key on? Instead of rewriting everything, our free case converter tool allows you to instantly transform your text to any format you need.

Available Text Cases:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters into capital letters.
  • lowercase: Converts all letters into small letters.
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of every word. Perfect for blog titles and headlines.
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of the first word in a sentence.
  • Alternating Case: Transforms text into a mix of upper and lower case letters (often used for internet memes).

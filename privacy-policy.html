Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Client-Side Processing (Your Data is Safe)

The core feature of our website is that all tools operate 100% client-side. This means that when you use tools like our JSON Formatter, Password Generator, or Word Counter, your data is processed directly inside your own web browser. We do not upload, store, or view your input data on any server.

2. Information We Collect

Since our tools run locally on your device, we do not require you to create an account, nor do we collect personal identification information such as your name, email, or address unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., via our Contact form).

3. Third-Party Advertising (Adsterra)

To keep this website free, we may use third-party advertising companies to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use aggregated information (not including your name, address, email address, or telephone number) about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

4. Cookies

We do not use our own cookies to track users. However, third-party services (such as advertisers or analytics tools) may place cookies on your browser to collect non-personal data.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Contact Page.

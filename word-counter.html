About the Free Word Counter Tool

Our online word counter is a perfect tool to check your text length instantly. Whether you are a student writing an essay, a professional crafting an email, or an SEO specialist optimizing content, this tool helps you stay within your character limits.

How do we calculate reading time?

The average adult reads at a speed of roughly 200 to 250 words per minute. Our tool estimates reading time based on a standard speed of 200 words per minute to give you a realistic expectation of how long it takes to read your text.

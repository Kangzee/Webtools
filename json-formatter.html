About Free JSON Formatter

Our free online JSON formatter and validator tool helps developers clean up messy, minified JSON data. It perfectly indents your code, making it highly readable. The tool runs 100% locally in your browser, meaning your sensitive data is never uploaded to any server.

How to use?

  • Copy your raw or minified JSON string.
  • Paste it into the left text area (Input).
  • Click the "Format / Beautify" button.
  • If your JSON is invalid, the validator will show the exact error.
